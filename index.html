S Class

Mercedes-Benz has launched the new-gen S-Class in the country. The model, which arrives in the country through the CBU route, is available in two variants that include the S450 and S400d. Under the hood, the Mercedes-Benz is offered with two powertrains that include 3.0-litre, six-cylinder petrol and diesel engines. The petrol motor is capable of producing 367bhp and 500Nm of torque, while the latter is tuned to produce 330bhp and 700Nm of torque. A nine-speed automatic transmission is standard across the line-up.

Maybach

Mercedes Benz India has launched the very first SUV in the Maybach range, known as the Mercedes-Maybach GLS 600 4Matic. It is the second Maybach model to be launched in the country after the Maybach S-Class. Dimension wise, the new Mercedes-Maybach GLS 600 4Matic measures 5,205mm in length, 2,030mm in width, and 1,838mm in height, while the wheelbase stands at 3,135mm. Exterior highlights of the model include 22-inch alloy wheels, a chrome grille with vertical slats and the Maybach logo, LED headlamps, LED tail lights, a chrome-finished B-pillar, and the Maybach logo on the D-pillar.

A Class

Mercedes-Benz A-Class Limousine Variants: It is available in three variants: 200, 200d, and AMG A35 4MATIC. Mercedes-Benz A-Class Limousine Powertrains: The A-Class Limousine is powered by 1.3-litre turbo-petrol and 2.0-litre diesel engines. The turbo-petrol unit puts out 163PS and 250Nm, while the diesel engine is good for 150PS and 320Nm. While the petrol engine is paired to a 7-speed DCT, the diesel engine is mated to an 8-speed DCT. The range-topping AMG variant gets a 2.0-litre turbo-petrol engine that belts out 306PS and 400Nm. The AMG, which gets a 7-speed DCT (dual-clutch automatic), comes with an AWD drivetrain.

C Class

The first two variants of the C-Class, the Prime and Progressive, make use of either a petrol or diesel engine. They are both 2.0-litre engines with the petrol engine putting out 203PS and 280Nm of torque, and the diesel putting out 194PS and 400Nm of torque. The AMG Line C300d uses the same diesel engine but it is tuned to make 245PS and 500Nm. All three engines here use a 9-speed automatic transmission. The C-Class Cabriolet uses a different 2.0-litre petrol engine that makes 258PS and 370Nm of torque.
